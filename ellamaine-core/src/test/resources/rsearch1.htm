17 NORTH LAKE ROAD ALFRED COVE
Features: Intercom, A/C
Beautiful three bedroom brick and tile character situated on green title block. This home has been re-wired, re-roofed, new plumbing and insulation, practically re-invented. The features include huge open plan lounge and dining, separate formal lounge or family room, bathroom with a sunken deep bath and skylight, r/c air-con, polished floorboards, timber skirting, country kitchen w/dishwasher, high ceilings, new timber exterior window frames. Electric gate with intercom for added privacy and security and much much more has been spent on this lovely cosy home. There is also a massive undercover carport for four cars, and large paved character brick coutyard for entertaining. Block size is approximatley 505 sqm. Call Michelle Eriksson on 0415 955 022 for further details.
